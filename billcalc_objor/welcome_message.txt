
Welcome to the Ting bill calculator. 
This program will take the minutes, messages and megabytes csv files (which can be downloaded from your Ting account each month) and can provide the following: 
(1) Full detail: usage & cost by person by type
(2) By person: usage & cost for one person by type
(3) By type: usage & cost by type

The first step will be to select what type of report you would like to run.
